It is the unit concerned with developing and commercializing innovative projects and ideas of a digital nature, which are being worked on in partnership with the College of Computer and Information Sciences.

The Objectives

Promoting the concept of technical innovation in the university community. Encouraging university students to present their technical ideas and transform them into innovations of economic value.

The Mission

To enable and support university employees, male and female, and faculty members, to build digital solutions based on scientific research and innovation to problems facing individuals or societies, ready to be launched or entered into the scientific market.

The Vision

Creating an integrated system that supports male and female students and faculty members at the university in their quest to discover the world of digital innovation
